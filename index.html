UX Design Process

Design is an interesting process of problem discovery and problem solving. I developed a streamlined and efficient design method to help me identify opportunities for innovation in the midst of the apparent chaos, and to transform these opportunities into actual design results.

Exploration and Analysis

Concept Transform

Intricate Design

Customer Interviews

This is the starting point for all projects - to have a heart-to-heart talk on the impending project with clints.

User Research

We delve into the users' lives, make observations, conduct interviews, and then construct points of view based on the users.

Design Analysis

Through design methods, competition analysis, user research, we redefine new design directions for the products.

Concept Proposal

Results of the contextual analyses are transformed into core values for the products. The points of views of the users that have been newly identified are thereafter carefully transformed into smooth, optimized interactive designs. But we do not stop here. Each design is in-built with its own unique elements and highlights, promising to deliver surprise after surprise.

Construction of Interactive Mode

As design highlights and requirements gradually take shape in the form of the actual service interface, we need to look into the operational characteristics of each platform and ensure seamless connection amongst the platforms. By focusing on people and simplifying the processes, we aim to develop consistent and tightly-knitted cross-platform experiences.

Detailed Flow Design

The framework of the main functions are confirmed, the process is executed page by page, and all contact points are meticulously connected.

Visual Design

Each brand is given distinctive characteristics such that visual style is consistent in every detail, and the design is perfected in every aspect.

Usability Testing

Prototype testing is employed to rapidly test product concepts. The resultant first-hand feedback helps me bring the products closer to market needs.
